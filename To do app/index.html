If you're unsure where to begin, simply start with the drawn card. Each task will be represented by a card, and all the cards together will form a deck, which will be shuffled. You can easily track your completed tasks. For larger tasks, such as "Prepare dinner," break them down into smaller tasks like "Peel potatoes" or "Boil water."
